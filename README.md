### What I do

I build AI systems that are meant to run in production, not just demo well. My focus is the layer most people skip: **agent orchestration, retrieval quality, evaluation, and cost.** Most of what I ship runs on free or low-cost model APIs by design — the constraint forces better architecture.

- 🧠 **Agents & orchestration** — multi-agent routing, tool use, and self-reflection loops with LangGraph
- 🔎 **Retrieval** — RAG pipelines with citations, re-ranking, and grounded answers (ChromaDB / pgvector)
- ⚙️ **Full-stack delivery** — FastAPI / Flask backends, React frontends, Docker, and CI to actually ship
- 💸 **Cost-aware by default** — production behaviour on a hobby budget

> Currently going deep on **financial AI** — turning unstructured financial data into decisions agents can act on.
